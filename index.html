Tata STRIVE - Ready for Cyber Security(R4CS)

Microsoft has partnered with Tata STRIVE to provide basic concepts of cyber security to everyone. Aim is to generate interest among students in his fast growing field. This is a self-paced course that introduces learners to the world of information and network security, device security, cyber laws, social engineering, identity management and most importantly career prospects in cyber security. Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks and data from malicious attacks. It is also known as information technology security or electronic information secutity.
